
Grognard folder is for "old style" strategy war-games.

____


grognard (plural grognards)

1. An old soldier.

2. A soldier of the original imperial guard that was created by Napoleon
I in 1804 and that made the final French charge at Waterloo.

3. (games, slang) Someone who enjoys playing older war-games or
roleplaying games, or older versions of such games, when newer ones are
available.
