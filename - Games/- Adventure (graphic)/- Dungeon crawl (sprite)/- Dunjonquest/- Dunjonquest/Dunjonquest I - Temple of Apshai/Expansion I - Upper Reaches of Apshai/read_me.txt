Upper Reaches of Apshai is the first expansion to Temple of Apshai It
contains four new dungeon levels for beginning characters, and conveys a
more humorous tone with suburban environments like a vegetable garden
and enemies like killer tomatoes.


HOW TO LOAD UPPER REACHES OF APSHAI - APPLE ][

Boot up your Temple of Apshai disk following the loading instructions
that came with your game. At the point where the computer asks you which
level you wish to explore, remove your Temple disk and insert The Upper
Reaches of Apshai disk. Type in a number between 1 and 4 and hit the
"Enter" key. You are now ready to begin play. At some point before
leaving the Dunjon, you must remove The Upper Reaches of Apshai disk and
put back in The Temple of Apshai disk.
