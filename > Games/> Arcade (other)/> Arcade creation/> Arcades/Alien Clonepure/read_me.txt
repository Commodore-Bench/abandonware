Alien Clonepure is a space shooter like many others. It is actually the
clone of a clone of a clone. It was created with Broderbund's Arcade
Machine but includes some modern gimmicks like an intro screen with
digitized audio and background Mockingboard music during gameplay. This
last feature is also compatible with the Echo+ sound card. To play using
the keyboard press B to switch the Paddles off and use K for left, L to
stop, ; for right and space to shoot.
